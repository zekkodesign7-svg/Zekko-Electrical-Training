Safe Work Practices
1. Daily Tool Box Talk & Work Alignment

DAILY MORNING MEETING: Before beginning any field tasks, every worker must attend the mandatory morning briefing. Use this time to clearly understand the specific scope of work assigned for the day and review the exact safety hazards associated with that work.

HAZARD AWARENESS: Listen closely to the specialized safety measures or precautions highlighted for your specific work area during the meeting before touching any tools or panels.

2. Adherence to Client & Site Safety Guidelines

STRICT COMPANY COMPLIANCE: Always follow the specific site safety guidelines and operational protocols established by the company or client facility where the work is being executed.

ZERO COMPROMISE: Local site rules, permit-to-work systems, and restricted zone boundaries must be respected without exception. Never bypass any client-mandated safety procedure to speed up an installation.
