Part 1: Daily Safety Gear Check (PPE) — Screen Text
1. HELMET: Wear it straight. Tighten the chin strap. Check for cracks.

2. SHOES: Thick insulating safety soles. Check for holes or damage.

3. GLOVES & SLEEVES: Wear them properly. No tears, cuts, or small holes.

4. GLASSES: Always wear clean safety glasses to protect your eyes.

5. REFLECTOR JACKET: Wear it neat and zipped up. Keep it clean and visible.
