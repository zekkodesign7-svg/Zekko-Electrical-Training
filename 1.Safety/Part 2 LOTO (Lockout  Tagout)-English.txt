Part 2: LOTO (Lockout / Tagout) & Panel Isolation
Why to Use:
It guarantees complete physical isolation from all live power sources, preventing accidental re-energization or electric shock while electrical termination work is being performed.

Where to Use:
This procedure must be applied at the main electrical isolation breaker or rotary switch of the industrial automation panel before opening the enclosure doors or handling any internal cabling.

Screen Text Points:

1. POWER OFF: Turn off the main power switch before you start working.

2. PERSONAL LOCK: Put your personal lock on the switch so no one else can turn it on.

3. LOTO TAG: Fix the standard LOTO tag directly onto your lock. Do not hang it loosely.

4. TAG DETAILS: Write your Name and Mobile Number clearly on the tag.

5. KEEP KEY: Keep the key in your pocket. Do not give it to anyone else.

Point and Call (Yosh!) Protocols:

6. APPLICATION CHECK: Point at the applied padlock and tag, verify alignment, and call out: "LOTO Applied, Switch Isolated, Safe to Work — Yosh!"

7. REMOVAL & RE-ENERGIZATION CHECK: After work is complete and tools are cleared, remove your lock, close the panel, point to verify the area is clear of people, and call out: "All Locks Removed, Area Clear, Safe to Energize — Yosh!"
